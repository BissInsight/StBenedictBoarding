Learn More About Our Journey

Our Benedictine Hostel is founded and managed by the fathers of St Thomas Benedictine Abbey Kappadu. The hostel started in 2001 exclusively for the male students of St.Antony's Public School.

  • Rooted in Benedictine spiritual values, fostering discipline, prayer, and community life.
  • Well-balanced, hygienic meals prepared to suit the health and preferences of resident students.
  • A nurturing atmosphere guided by experienced mentors and caretakers.

St.Benedictine Abbey

Vision

To be a beacon of prayer, peace, and service, radiating the Benedictine spirit in the heart of the community.

Leadership / Governance

Guided by the Abbot and monastic community, our leadership follows the principles of discernment, humility, and shared responsibility, ensuring decisions align with our spiritual and communal values.

Mission

Rooted in the Rule of St. Benedict, we commit ourselves to a life of prayer, work, and hospitality, fostering spiritual growth, education, and compassion for all.

Spiritual Focus

Centered on the Eucharist, Liturgy of the Hours, and contemplative prayer, we seek to deepen our relationship with God and serve as instruments of His peace and love.
