Learn More About Our Journey

Our Benedictine Hostel is founded and managed by the fathers of St Thomas Benedictine Abbey Kappadu. The hostel started in 2001 exclusively for the male students of St.Antony's Public School.

  • Rooted in Benedictine spiritual values, fostering discipline, prayer, and community life.
  • Well-balanced, hygienic meals prepared to suit the health and preferences of resident students.
  • A nurturing atmosphere guided by experienced mentors and caretakers.
